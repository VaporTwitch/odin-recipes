Quorn Fillets with Lemon Broccolini and Baby Potatoes

Description

This recipe combines the convenience of pre-made Quorn Pesto Fillets with the freshness of lemon broccolini and baby potatoes. It's a delicious and easy-to-make vegetarian meal that can be ready in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of Quorn Pesto Fillets
  • 1 bunch of broccolini
  • 1 pound baby potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat your oven to the temperature recommended on the Quorn Pesto Fillets package. Cook as instructed.
    2. Prepare the baby potatoes: Wash the baby potatoes and cut any larger ones in half so that they are roughly the same size. Place them in a pot of salted water and bring it to a boil. Cook the potatoes until they are fork-tender, which usually takes about 10-15 minutes. Drain the potatoes and set them aside.
    3. Prepare the broccolini: Trim the tough ends of the broccolini and discard them. In a large bowl, toss the broccolini with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
    4. Roast the broccolini: Place the seasoned broccolini on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or aluminum foil. Roast them in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes or until they are tender and slightly crispy at the edges.
    5. Sauté the baby potatoes: In a large skillet, heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the cooked baby potatoes and sauté them for a few minutes until they are slightly crispy and golden.
    6. Assemble the dish: Place the Quorn Pesto Fillets on a serving plate. Arrange the lemon broccolini and sautéed baby potatoes alongside the fillets.
    7. Serve: Optionally, you can sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese over the broccolini and potatoes for extra flavor.

    Now your Quorn Pesto Fillets with Lemon Broccolini and Baby Potatoes are ready to be served. It's a nutritious and satisfying meal that brings together the flavors of pesto, lemon, and fresh vegetables. Enjoy!
