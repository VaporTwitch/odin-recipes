Isaly's BBQ Chipped Ham Sandwich

Description

Isaly's was a popular regional chain of dairy stores and restaurants known for their chipped chopped ham sandwich with a unique BBQ sauce. Here's a recipe to recreate that classic Isaly's BBQ chipped ham sandwich:

Ingredients

For the BBQ sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the sandwich:

  • 1 pound chipped ham (You can find chipped ham at some delis or use shaved deli ham.)
  • 8 hamburger buns or your favorite sandwich rolls

Optional Toppings:

  • Sliced pickles
  • Coleslaw

Instructions

  1. To make the BBQ sauce, in a medium saucepan, combine the ketchup, apple cider vinegar, water, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, prepared yellow mustard, onion powder, garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine.
  2. Place the saucepan over medium heat and bring the mixture to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken slightly.
  3. While the BBQ sauce is simmering, warm the chipped ham in a skillet or microwave until heated through.
  4. Once the BBQ sauce is ready, remove it from the heat and set it aside.
  5. To assemble the sandwiches, take a hamburger bun or sandwich roll and open it up.
  6. Place a generous amount of the warmed chipped ham on one side of the bun.
  7. Drizzle a spoonful or two of the prepared BBQ sauce over the chipped ham.
  8. If desired, add some sliced pickles and coleslaw on top of the ham and BBQ sauce.
  9. Close the sandwich with the other half of the bun.
  10. Repeat the process to make more sandwiches.

Enjoy your homemade Isaly's BBQ chipped ham sandwiches! These sandwiches are a delicious combination of flavors, and the tangy BBQ sauce complements the savory chipped ham perfectly. It's a taste of nostalgia for those who remember Isaly's, and a delightful treat for anyone looking to try this classic regional sandwich.
